Adventure Time is a Cartoon Network children's show that was created by Pendleton Ward and aired from 2010 to 2018. If you want to learn more, Click Here

This show was intended for audiences of children of all ages covering both soft and fun morals to dark and tragic themes. The intention of the show is both to be an enjoyable romp through Ooo and a gateway to heavy topics and tragic characters.
